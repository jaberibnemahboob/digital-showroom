about us

Our basic philosophy is to create something that appeals to people in their own special way - which adds something extra to them without adding anything superfluous. In our design we draw on nomadism - the idea of ​​always walking and being prepared to move on - and to do it with pleasure. Therefore, our designs are designed to fit into different contexts and to be easily moved. They will stand out in any interior, like objects that create both comfort and joy in our everyday lives.

“Quality in design is about choosing the right material and the right level of manufacturing for the respective purpose.”

Claus Jensen & Henrik Holbæk

-designers

With more than 300 Danish and international design prices, Claus Jensen and Henrik Holbæk, also known as Tools®, are among the most award-winning designers in Europe. Their products are known for their simplicity and ingenuity, often with a little extra or surprising functionality. The designers have created most of the products in the brand Eva Solo.

workshop

Our office, product development department and photo studio are located in Fredensborg, north of Copenhagen, where the Danish Queen has one of his residences. The building dates from 1857 and served as a detention house and court building for 100 years. Outstanding is that the house of arrest is still almost unchanged from that time and we therefore feel privileged that since 2013 we have had all 13 cells as well as the other facilities of the place and at the same time a neighbor to the Queen's Garden.
