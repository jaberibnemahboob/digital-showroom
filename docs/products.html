Comments from Designers

Usually you think of furniture as something that is stationary. But the most used furniture in the home is often the ones that move around. They become the practical assistants who fill the space around the stationary sofas and armchairs. Pilgrim is a series of shovels and a side table that can easily be arranged as needed. And to emphasize their flowability, we have provided them with a hanger. It clearly tells you that there is something that you want to move on, and it's also practical.

Design by Tools® Henrik Holbæk & Claus Jensen
