The Girrafe Clothes Rack

1460,00 DKK

A clothes rack in a design without any visible joining parts like screws or other fittings has resulted in a construction that has created additional possibility for using the clothes rack not only with hangers but also as hooks. Though named Giraffe the height is at a level where it is being a part of the interior without dominating it.

Comments from Designers

Normally, you do not walk around and look forward to the thought that your furniture can be taken apart and packed flat. On the other hand, it is the designers’ challenge to make a virtue out of necessity. In the case of the clothes rack, this applies more than for any other product. There are very few elements that form a large piece of furniture and therefore it is impossible to transport it if it cannot be taken apart. So, when you put the clothes rack together for the first time, we hope you will enjoy how beautifully the elements work together. And every day you can be happy that the next time you move, it will be easy to take along.

Design by Tools® Henrik Holbæk & Claus Jensen
